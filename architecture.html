Component Roles


1. **Main Loop (`main.py`)**: Orchestrates the entire process. It decides when to call other modules and manages the application state (loaded from `bot_state.json`).

2. **BinanceConnector (`binance_api.py`)**: Handles all low-level communication with Binance (fetching prices, candles, balances, and placing orders). Supports a "Demo Mode" for safety.

3. **EstrategiaConfluencia (`strategy.py`)**: The "Brain". Analyzes candle data using technical indicators (EMA, RSI, ADX, Volume) to generate buy signals.

4. **RiskManager (`risk.py`)**: The "Safety Guard".

* **Daily Limit**: Stops trading if losses exceed 3% for the day.

* **Position Sizing**: Calculates trade size to risk max 1% of equity based on stop-loss distance.

5. **NewsAnalyzer (`sentiment.py`)**: Fetches the "Fear & Greed Index" to act as a market sentiment filter (prevents buying during extreme panic).

6. **TrailingStopATR (`risk.py`)**: Manages the exit strategy. It raises the stop-loss price as the market goes up, locking in profits, based on market volatility (ATR).
